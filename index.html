ub data science research lab

The University at Buffalo Data Science (UBDS) research group is located in the Computer Science and Engineering department at the University at Buffalo. We develop machine learning based data science solutions for problems in a wide variety of domains, including cyber-security, threat detection, healthcare, and sustainability.

I am on a temporary leave from UB for next few years, and will be serving as a Program Director in the Office of Advanced Cyberinfrastructure, at the National Science Foundation. I will not be taking any new students until then.
